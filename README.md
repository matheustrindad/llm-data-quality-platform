\## 🚨 The "Garbage In, Garbage Out" Killer for LLMs



In the era of LLMs and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), the cost of dirty data is higher than ever. Training or fine-tuning models on inconsistent, duplicate, or low-context data burns compute and degrades performance.



This project is a \*\*production-grade simulation\*\* of an \*\*AI Data Platform\*\*. It doesn't just move data from A to B; it \*\*evaluates\*\* it using three layers of defense before allowing it to reach the Data Mart or Training Pipeline.



> \*\*Differentiator:\*\* Custom \*\*LLM-as-a-Judge\*\* evaluation engine that assesses data fitness for AI training contexts.
